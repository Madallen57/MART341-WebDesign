Our Story

Our Beginnings

GreenBloom started in 2010 as a small community project in a neighborhood park. Our mission was simple: empower individuals with knowledge and tools to live sustainably.

Our Growth

Over the years, GreenBloom has grown into a nonprofit organization hosting over 50 workshops annually, with hundreds of volunteers contributing to our success.

Our Mission

We aim to inspire and educate communities to adopt sustainable practices, fostering a healthier planet for future generations.
