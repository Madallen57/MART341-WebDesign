How to Make Perfect S'mores

  • TIPS
  • PERFECTION

Ingredients

  • 6 Graham crackers
  • 1-2 Chocolate bars
  • 6 Marshmallows

Steps

  1. Preheat your campfire or broiler.
  2. Break graham crackers in half.
  3. Place a square of chocolate on one half and a marshmallow on top.
  4. Toast the marshmallow until golden and gooey.
  5. Sandwich with another cracker and enjoy!
